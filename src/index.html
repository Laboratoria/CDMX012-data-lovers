Ghibli World

Ghibli Worldis an encyclopedia of all the works of Studio Ghibli's. The studio is best known for its animated feature films, such as Princess Mononoke, Spirited Away and Howl's Moving Castle. These films exemplify the master story telling of environmentalism, friendship, love, family, culture and war.
