Studio Ghibli

It is a Japanese animation film studio based in Koganei, Tokyo. Founded in 1985 by animators and directors Miyazaki Hayao and Takahata Isao and producer Suzuki Toshio. Studio Ghibli is known for the high quality of its cinema and its art. His feature films won praise from both critics and popularity and influenced other animation studios.
