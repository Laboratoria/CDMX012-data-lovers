STUDIO GHIBLI

The studio was founded on June 15, 1985 by the directors Hayao Miyazaki, Isao Takahata and the producer Toshio Suzuki. It is a japanese animation film studio and it is currently best known as one of the best animation film studios of all times. Some of the Studio Ghibli’s films have been nominated in International Film Festivals like the Academy Awards for Best Animated Feature. Among the films nominated are: Spirited Away (2001), Howl’s Moving Castle (2004), The Wind Rises (2013), The Tale of the Princess Kaguya (2013) and When Marnie was there (2014). Spirited Away won the Golden Bear in 2002 and the 2003 Academy Aawrds for Best Animated Feature.
